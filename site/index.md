# JD Grab

Select and copy job description text on LinkedIn, Indeed, Glassdoor, Wellfound,
and Welcome to the Jungle with one keystroke.

## What it does

JD Grab adds a keyboard shortcut (and right-click menu) that selects the full
job description on a supported job posting page and copies it to your
clipboard — no dragging, no partial selections.

## Supported sites

- LinkedIn
- Indeed
- Glassdoor
- Wellfound
- Welcome to the Jungle

## Privacy

JD Grab does not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data. See the full
[Privacy Policy](./privacy-policy/).
